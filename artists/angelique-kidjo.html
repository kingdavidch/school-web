Angelique Kidjo

Biography

Angelique Kidjo is a Nigerian-born, French-raised singer, songwriter, and activist known for her powerful voice and advocacy for social justice. She has been a prominent figure in the global music scene since the late 1980s and has collaborated with numerous artists across different genres.
